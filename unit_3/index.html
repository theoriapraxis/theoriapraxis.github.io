Let the theoria and praxis begin.

We believe ideas come to life best through good design and testing. So we created Theoria ('thinking') and Praxis ('doing') as an experience design collective to work on ideas by carefully thinking through the design process and doing by building workable prototypes for user testing. We specialize in:

  • Experience design
  • Prototype development
  • User testing
